I'm passionate about ART and TECHNOLOGY

As a Project Manager turned UX/UI Designer, I love bridging the gap between people and technology. My background in graphic design and web development gives me a unique perspective on how thoughtful design and strategic execution come together to create intuitive digital experiences.


After earning my degree in Graphic Design, I spent several years building a career in project management, where I learned how to balance creativity with business needs. Later, completing a full stack web development bootcamp reignited my passion for hands-on design and helped me connect my technical and creative sides.


At Comcast, I’ve led projects that prioritize user experience while meeting organizational goals, and I’ve learned the value of empathy, collaboration, and iteration in every product decision.


I coded this portfolio with the aid of ChatGPT as both a creative challenge and a statement: design should be as functional as it is beautiful. My goal is to continue designing experiences that feel effortless, meaningful, and human.
